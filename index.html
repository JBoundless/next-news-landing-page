About Us

We are a news app based out of Philadelphia, PA, that seeks to distribute hard-hitting and honest journalism to our users from newspapers such as The New York Times, The Philadelphia Inquirer, and The Boston Globe. We also provide news on a variety of other topics, such as entertainment and sports. We even offer original NextNews conent from writers based in the U.S. and all over the world. There are different plans available, and signing up is fast and easy. The app is free to download.

PLEASE NOTE: Free users are limited to ten articles per month. No re-subscription is necessary unless a paid plan is desired.
